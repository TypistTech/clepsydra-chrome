FAQs

How to open a faucet?

You don't! Clepsydra auto-open faucets periodically according to your timer settings.

Note that Clepsydra only open the faucets which available for claims. For example: Clepsydra only open Moon Litecon once within 5 minutes because you can't get a second claim from it.

If you must open the faucets manually, wait for the next version (0.2.x). You will able to open a faucet by clicking the Clepsydra icon on the top right corner.

How to add a new faucet?

Faucets are hand-picked by the extension author. You can suggest one via clepsydra@typist.tech

Basic requirements

  • No more than 2 captcha for one claim (one for login; one for claim)
  • No terminator level captcha
  • No annoying pop-up / pop-under / redirects
  • Reasonable withdrawal threshold

Extra points

  • Use a microwallet
  • Have a daily bonus program (for example: the Moon franchises)
  • Direct payout to microwallet (for example: StarBits and Fautsy)
  • Use HTTPS everywhere
  • Have a way to earn with minimum actions (for example: KickassTraffic and BitVideo)
Life is too short. No matter it accepted or not, you won't get a reply. Thanks in advance.

What is microwallet?

Since each faucet payment takes time and miner fee, it's impractical for faucet owners to pay you for each claim. Therefore, faucet owners send your coins to a microwallet. This usually completes within minutes and free of charge until you withdraw. Popular microwallets:

  • FaucetHub
  • CoinPot
